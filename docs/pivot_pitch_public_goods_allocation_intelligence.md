## The one-line idea
**An open, agentic AI system that finds value the public sector already set aside for
communities — and routes it, transparently, to affordable housing.**

## Why this is *ultra-aligned* (not just on-mission — literally our articles)
Hive Life Haven Inc.'s **filed corporate purpose** is:
- *Relief of poverty* → affordable housing initiatives for people in need.
- *Advancement of education* → training in **emerging technologies** (AI, robotics).

The Forager serves **both clauses at once**: it uses agentic AI (the education/innovation
purpose) to *fund* affordable housing (the poverty-relief purpose). It is not a tangent —
it is the mechanical link between our two legal purposes. That is rare alignment, and it is
verifiable on Corporations Canada.

## Why this is *ultra-elegant*
- **One permissive core.** Apache-2.0. Any community org can fork it. No lock-in.
- **One transparent ledger.** Every discovered opportunity, every dollar routed, every
  outcome — in an open, auditable trail (`tracking_ledger.py`).
- **One deployable artifact.** Live on Railway today; source public on GitHub.
- **Zero extraction.** The system harvests *already-allocated public value* (grants owed,
  rebates unclaimed, funding notices unread) — it does not ask the community to pay.

## Why this is *ultra-intelligent*
- Autonomous discovery across fragmented civic systems: government grant portals, pilot-program
  notices, public-goods funding rounds, unclaimed-property registries.
- LLM-driven relevance scoring + human-in-the-loop verification before any action.
- A digest layer that turns raw discovery into a *decision-ready* brief for humans.
