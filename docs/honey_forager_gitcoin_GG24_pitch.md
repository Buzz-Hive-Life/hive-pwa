## The Honey Forager (flagship feature)
An open agentic subsystem inside the app that:
- Scans public funding sources (grants, scholarships, RetroPGF rounds) for opportunities the community is eligible for.
- Drafts applications from *verified* corporate facts (no invented claims).
- Routes recovered value to Hive Life Haven Inc.'s affordable-housing mission.
- Runs kill-switch-gated and fully logged — safe, auditable, open.

## Problem
Non-profits and communities leave enormous value unclaimed (expired grant rounds, unredeemed rebates, lapsed public-goods funding, free compute). Human bandwidth can't track it. Meanwhile affordable housing — a core public good — is starved of capital. Members also lack a coherent interface to their cooperative's services.

## Solution
One assistant that (a) serves members day-to-day and (b) autonomously forages overlooked public value back to the cooperative. Open code, member-governed, 100% revenue reinvested into mission.

## Public good delivered
- **Affordable housing** cooperative infrastructure (target: 55.5% non-market housing by 2055).
- **Open agentic tooling** anyone can fork to recover overlooked community value.
- **Member empowerment** for an underserved community (housing-coop members).
